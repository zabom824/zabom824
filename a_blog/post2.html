Not Lovin' It

Ah, food. We can't live without it...literally. Since the struggle for survival has long since been won here in North America and in other developed countries, food has become so much more than an essential in our daily lives. It has become a pastime, a friend, a source of entertainment, and a main ingredient in the success of many multi-million dollar fast food chains. Notice how I said "a main" instead of THE main. This is due to there being several other factors that contribute to the success of all these big league fast-food chains- McDonald's, Wendy's, Subway, you name it. In today's world, these factors include identity design, branding, advertising, etc.

I'm not gonna lie, McDonald's makes some damn good fries. And some seriously killer McNuggets (seriously—a girl named Stacy Irvine nearly lost her life after collapsing from eating nothing but goddamn McNuggets for fifteen years!) Yes, fast food tastes good, and I'm not one of those health-obsessed people that shun the existence of fast food. I'm here to talk about design. And boy, does McDonald's have an iconic design. In fact, in 2014 McDonald's was ranked the 6th most important brand in the world.
