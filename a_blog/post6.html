Music

Music is, without a doubt, a huge source of inspiration and motivation for me. I love many different genres, ranging from classical music to industrial rock. Anything from Mozart to Marilyn Manson is what I like to say. I'm one of those people that can proudly state that music has helped me through really tough times. It didn't exactly save my life, no—that was the work of people very close to me, but it sure helped make my life worth living.

The first job I ever dreamed of in terms of design was to create album covers, posters, and merchandise for bands. This is still my greatest aspiration, my ultimate goal. I know very little about designing for the music industry, but I'm very eager to experience it and learn. I know typography, which I assume is going to be very necessary. Ah, just thinking about band logos livens me up.

I guess I'm looking for to be a part of something that is so important in my life. I want to do the thing for other people that music did for me, although indirectly because I would be working on the visuals of the band rather than the sound. I would start a band, but I only know how to play piano, and my singing skills are quite rusty due to years of no practice. Who knows? Maybe I will start one in the near future. I've already designed a logo for this hypothetical band, which by the way is called Scairytale, just like my website.
