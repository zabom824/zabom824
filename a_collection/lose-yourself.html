"Lose Yourself" is a hit rap single released in 2002 by Eminem. It contains the memorable line "Mom's spaghetti", leading to its usage in spaghetti stories that usually start out as a personal anecdote about an embarrassing or socially awkward episode before ending abruptly with a sudden appearance of spaghetti.
