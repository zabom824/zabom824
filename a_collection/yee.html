Yee, 2015
Video

“Yee” is an onomatopoeia taken from a video featuring an animated dinosaur specified as Ankylosaurus called Peek singing a small jingle, only to be interrupted at the end by a Tyrannosaurus Rex named Oro shouting "Yee!". Photoshopped images and video remixes of the dinosaur began circulating in August 2014 after the video surfaced on Reddit.
